What is skimboarding

The last ten
meters

Drop the board on wet sand, run, jump on, ride out to meet the wave — then ride it back. No paddling, no lineup.

Every trick happens within meters of the beach — and of your banner.

The story — Tommy & MIK

Built from
scratch, by hand

Tommy grew up on a mining island with no surf. As a merchant sailor he saw the world's beaches — then found waves back home nobody rode, until he typed his shore break into Google and taught himself to skim.

MIK today

Boards, lessons,
milkshakes

A board workshop and shop in Canggu, a skim school where local and visiting kids learn the sport, and a small beach kitchen making milkshakes for surfers. Built from scratch.

Why the Gathering

Show Bali
the sport

Skimboarding barely exists in Indonesia — a handful of riders on an island with thousands of kilometers of perfect skim beaches. The Gathering changes that: showing the sport to Bali's kids, families, and beach crowd — with the best riders in Asia doing the showing.
